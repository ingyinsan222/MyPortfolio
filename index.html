About

I'm a student who got hooked on the moment a design stops being a picture and starts being something you can click, drag, and break. Most of what I know came from taking things apart — other people's sites, my own half-finished projects — and rebuilding them a little better each time.

Right now I'm splitting my time between coursework and personal builds like this one, chasing the small details: the way a panel catches light, the timing of a hover, the moment a page loads and doesn't feel like every other page.
